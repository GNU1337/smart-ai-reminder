# 🌅 Daglig Briefing: The Cognitive Lifeline for Dignity, Memory, and Love

> **"What if your most precious thoughts, promises, and connections didn’t drift away with the wind?"**

Imagine waking up every day in a state of quiet panic. The port code was written down somewhere, but the paper is gone. You’re supposed to take your medication, but was it two pills or one? You received a warm text from your wife, Anna, but your phone’s notification tray is an overwhelming, flashing battlefield of advertisements, news alerts, and spam, hiding her message entirely. This isn't just about forgetting; it is about the heartbreaking friction of losing control of your own narrative—a kognitiv stress that slowly erodes your confidence, your relationships, and your independence.

**Daglig Briefing (Daily Briefing)** is not just another app in a saturated market of cold, clinical "productivity" systems. **It is an exquisite, compassionate digital temple.** It is a personalized cognitive sanctuary designed from the ground up for people experiencing memory challenges, along with the loved ones who stand beside them. It restores autonomy. It cures overwhelm. It bridges the gap between love and technology.

This is the pitch for a product that doesn't just manage data—**it saves peace of mind.**

## 🖤 Why This Matters (The Human Core)

When someone suffers from memory loss, traditional tech tools are a hostile environment. Small fonts, endless nested menus, relentless blinking alarms, and complex keyboards feel like cognitive mountains. 

We threw out the standard Silicon Valley playbook. We engineered a workspace that feels like **a soft, warm cup of tea on a quiet morning**. 

Every feature is carefully tuned to provide **high-contrast, eye-safe, and low-stimulant comfort**:
* **Zero Cognitive Noise:** Only what you need, exactly when you need it. High-contrast typography paired with generous negative space.
* **Swedish Sincerity:** Built naturally in Swedish, speaking in a warm, encouraging, human voice rather than clinical, robotic system messages.
* **The "Anna" Filter:** A dedicated priority recognition algorithm that scans your daily digital flows to put messages, notes, and dates from loved ones right at the top.

## 🌟 The Features That Redefine Independence

### 🌅 1. Beautiful, Intelligent Recaps (Morgon, Kväll & Vecka)
Powered by the state-of-the-art **Gemini 3.5**, Daglig Briefing extracts the heartbeat of your notifications, bookmarks, and memo bank to deliver highly legible, stress-free text reports. 
* **Morgonsammanfattning (Morning):** Softly briefs you on what’s ahead today, what medicines to take, and what Anna wrote.
* **Kvällssammanfattning (Evening):** A soothing wind-down wrapping up the day’s achievements and ensuring peace of mind before bed.
* **Veckorapport (Weekly Retro):** A brand new long-term retro summarizing the structure of your week. It highlights your daily progress, tracks regular activities, and builds deep patterns for a worry-free weekend.

### 🎙️ 2. Swedish Voice Dictation (Speak, Don't Fret)
Typing on tiny mobile keyboards can be deeply frustrating. With **Web Speech API integration**, users can simply tap a charming coral microphone icon, speak naturally in Swedish (*"Koden till cykelrummet är fjorton nittiotvå"*), and watch their thoughts automatically form into beautiful, permanent text cards. No typing, no spelling mistakes, no stress.

### ⏰ 3. Glowing Time-Based Alarms & Synthesis
For critical notes—like exact morning pills or heavy laundry times—users can bind a specific clock. When the time comes:
* A striking, pulsing neon-blue screen alert rises.
* A pleasing, gentle browser sound chime is synthesized to firmly but softly grab attention.
* The note automatically promotes itself to a priority alert, ensuring it is never buried.

### 📱 4. Notification Filtering & Page Trackers
Say goodbye to spam. This app functions as a clean, virtual simulator for incoming alerts. It parses feed items (Calendar, News, Anna) and filters out the useless clutter, so only the critical life details remain visible. Connects seamlessly with custom curated bookmarks (SMHI, SVT) to fetch outside information without forcing the user to navigate the scary, open web.

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack (Under the Hood)

Crafted with premium developer hygiene, the app is blazing fast, highly performant, and robust:

* **Frontend:** React 18+ with TypeScript, running on **Vite** for near-instant boot times.
* **Layout & Fluid Animations:** Tailwind CSS for custom responsive slates, complemented by buttery, hardware-accelerated fluid transitions powered by `motion` (Framer Motion).
* **The Intelligence Engine:** **Express Node.js backend** leveraging the modern **@google/genai SDK** with standard server-side token protection. No API keys are ever leaked to the client.
* **Sensory APIs:** Integrated **HTML5 Web Speech API** for hands-free Swedish voice detection and the **Web Audio API** for real-time oscillator chime synthesis.

## 🚀 Experience the Calm (Setup Guide)

To run this product locally and witness the future of compassionate tech:

1. **Clone the Repo:**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/yourusername/daglig-briefing.git
   cd daglig-briefing
   ```

2. **Supply the Intelligence (Secrets):**
   Create a `.env` file in the root directory:
   ```env
   GEMINI_API_KEY=your_secured_google_gemini_api_key_here
   ```

3. **Install & Run:**
   ```bash
   npm install
   npm run dev
   ```
   Open your browser to `http://localhost:3000` and take a deep breath.
